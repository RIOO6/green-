Welcome to Sudanese Green Crescent Society

About Us

The Sudanese Green Crescent Society, established in 2017 under the Sudanese Aid Commission Act, is a national non-profit organization dedicated to promoting healthy behaviors and preventing addiction to tobacco, drugs, alcohol, and other harmful substances. We focus on raising awareness and supporting vulnerable groups—especially children, youth, mothers, and students—through health education and prevention programs. Our mission is to safeguard communities and reduce the health impact of addiction, both in everyday life and during emergencies.
